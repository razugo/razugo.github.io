# Introduction to Liquid Hacks

So a couple weeks ago I participated in Liquid Hacks a hackathan hosted by the esports organization Team Liquid. It pretty unique for an esports org to have a hackathon in the first place so I thought it would be a good opportunity to participate. The turnout was really good and I was supprised to see so many creative projects. I'll link the devpost below

[Devpost](https://liquidhacks.devpost.com/)

For our project we decided to build Outdrafted, a machine learning based application that gives a probability of one team composition winning against another. We were inspired to make this becuase of the shear amount of league that I and my teammate play (lmao). This tool would be a fun way to cross gaming with technology.
