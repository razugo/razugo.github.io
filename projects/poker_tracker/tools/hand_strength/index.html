How to Read This Chart

Grid Layout

  • Diagonal: Pocket pairs (AA, KK, QQ, etc.)
  • Upper triangle: Suited hands (AKs, QJs, etc.)
  • Lower triangle: Offsuit hands (AKo, QJo, etc.)

Range Selection

  • Slider/Input: Adjust percentage of hands to highlight
  • Blue highlighting: Shows selected range of strongest hands
  • Percentile view: Toggle to see exact strength rankings
