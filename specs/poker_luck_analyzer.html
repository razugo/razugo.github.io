🎲 Poker Hand Luck Analyzer

Analyze your dealt hands to see if you're low rolling, hitting expected values, or high rolling

Supported Formats:
• Standard: AhKs, QdJc, 7h2s (suits: h=hearts, d=diamonds, c=clubs, s=spades)
• Alternative: AK, QJ, 72 (suited hands will be detected automatically)
• Multiple hands: Separate with commas, spaces, or new lines
• Example: AhKs, QdJc, 72o, AKs, JJ, 93s
